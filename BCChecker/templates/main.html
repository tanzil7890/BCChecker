Welcome to BCChecker

BCChecker is a tool created by Mohammad Tanzil Idrisi available for medical oncologists, professionals, and medical, aiming to reduce the amount of false positives, false negatives and overdiagnoses after mammograms. With BCChecker, you guarantee yourself another layer of certainity. Simply enter patient/lesion data below to receive a result (benignancy or malignancy)

Legend

Shape: 1 for round, 2 for oval, 3 for lobular, 4 for irregular

Mass margin: 1 for circumscribed, 2 for microlobulated, 3 for obscured, for ill-defined, 5 for spiculated

Density: 1 for high, 2 for iso, 3 for low, 4 for fat-containing
